House cat protector

This House cat protector is used to keep cats that want to pick a fight with your cat away from your house, it uses a sensor to identify when a bad cat goes past the line, and then makes a sound that cats hate to scare it off. but it lets your cat pass without hearing the sound because of the collar that it would be wearing, the collar has a RFID (radio-frequency identification) chip in it. Having this collar it can pass through the field without making the sounds, so it can go home without a scare.

This would be used by pet owners who have evil cats in the area that keep beating up their cat and they would like to keep it safer.

To make this you would need a loudspeaker, bluetooth, waterproofing material to be the case for it, and it would need software, C would be the best choice for software, it can keep it small.

There are some challenges that would need to be overcome:

  • Having it smaller so people can carry it
  • Having the collar part small enough not to annoy the cat
  • Having the computer parts smaller
  • Keep cost down
  • Weatherproofing for the sensor and collar
  • Identifying the correct sound to keep cats away
  • Having a Low battery sensor for the cat collar
  • Manufacturing it
  • Learning C
